The Linux Kernel

By far the most used kernel besides WinNT and Darwin.

Created by Finnish developer Linus Torvalds, it became a pretty big deal in the OS market, being loved by Valve, and it being used lots on servers, but Linux is also good on Desktop computers, since its open source and usually safer than Windows.
