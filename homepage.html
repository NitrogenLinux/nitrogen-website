What do you mean by "Performance, Simplified"?
"Performance, Simplified" means Nitrogen is made for performance, but made simple, without much optimization needed from the user.

Is Nitrogen Portable?
No. Due to Elements creating your own set of optimization(not implemented yet), that work only on your specific hardware, if you upgrade, you can always recompile the whole system with lmt --recompile(-r).
